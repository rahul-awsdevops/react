Explore Goa

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is a popular beach in Goa known for its vibrant nightlife, water sports, and shacks serving delicious seafood.

Fort Aguada

Fort Aguada is a historic fort offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and is a popular tourist destination in North Goa.

Palolem Beach

Palolem Beach is a beautiful crescent-shaped beach in South Goa, known for its clear water and peaceful environment.

Dudhsagar Waterfalls

Dudhsagar Waterfalls is one of the tallest waterfalls in India, located on the border of Goa and Karnataka.
